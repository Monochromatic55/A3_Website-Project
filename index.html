THE BREAK DOWN

KEY POINTS

THE JUICY STUFF

The Problem: Launched in 2020, Varsity’s loyalty mobile app “Varsity League” has currently gained over 34,000+ plus users from its initial debut; it's unknown if these users are active/inactive.

With its current fanbase, Varsity is looking for a solution that would increase user engagement amongst Varsity users, the individual stores, and the brand itself.

The solution should strength the culture and sense of community at Varsity in-order to increase brand loyalty and perception.

WHO IS VARSITY

First opened in 2013, Nedlands, WA. Varsity introduced themselves as an American college “frat-style” themed bar that offered cheap drinks and American “over-the-top” styled food. Due to their venue’s first location, their dominate demographic were the young university students of UWA (The University of Western Australia). Since 2013, Varsity has opened 7 new locations across WA. In response to their rapid development and growth, Varsity now advertises itself as a classic American style sports bar that catered towards university students, families, American food-fans, and sports fans for average Western Australian audience.

1

The main focus should be centred on how we can: “hone onto our current members and strength their engagement while leaning towards creating a tighter community”.

Key words include: “community” “culture” “engagement” “interaction”

2

The current mobile application includes features such as:
Event Calendar: Users are notified of upcoming events.
Exclusive Discounts: Special discounts you earn by using the app.
Secret Menu Items: Exclusive options you can only get through the app.
Online Ordering: Includes both food and merchandise.
Stamp cards: Earn freebies by regularly eating at Varsity.

3

The solution should be able to fit into the existing app with ease. There’s no need to re-create the app UNLESS it’s in regards to fixing pre-existing issues or implementing new features that would be beneficial to the users. This brief also allows for the implementation of physical materials that would complement the app (physical loyalty cards, QR scanning codes, etc).
